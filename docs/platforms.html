Platforms

Missing any information? Wondering if you're behind? Check these sites to remain on top of everything!


Central


Quest

Your centre console for admission information and course selection, stay up to date with Quest to keep track of deadlines, complete needed tasks on time and keep you place at the university!


WatIAM

Responsible for usernames and password across all platforms affiliated with the University of Waterloo.


Academics


LEARN

An online course program currently used for introductory courses to Waterloo's online academic system.


ALEKS

A learning module for first-year engineering students to self-assess and refresh math skills. All student information on this platform cannot be accessed by the university, but its completion is highly recommended for the school year.


Mobius

An online platform used to provide content to LEARN classrooms. Mobius is set up once a student has access to Waterloo LEARN.


Co-op


CareerHub

Visit this site for info and tips on the co-op process, and how it relates to your program. Need help with your resume? Check this site out!


WaterlooWorks

Waterloo's central for job boards, which provide opportunities for co-op students and alumni.


Collaboration


Office 365

All your Microsoft programs, provided by Waterloo and kept all in one place! Used primarily and a schedule, to collaborate with your teams and for its email function.


Piazza

A classroom-type forum where instructors and fellow students can answer often anonymous questions. This is integral for many online classes.


WaterlooLEADS

LEADS allows you to register and keep track of your events.


UWflow

UW student reviews of courses, professors, and several subjects.


Video Conferencing


Microsoft Teams

Linked to Office 365 accounts, many profs use Teams for meetings. This platform is especially helpful for smaller groups.


Zoom

One of the world’s leading video conferencing platforms, also used for classes and meetings within the University.


Cisco Webex

Another online meeting platform used by the University, especially during the information talks before school started.


Bongo

An online teaching platform that minimizes student input to messages to reduce distractions.


Apps


Portal

Waterloo’s student digital assistant, used to book appointments and keep track of all your platforms!


Not affliated with the University of Waterloo
